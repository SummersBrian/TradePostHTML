Developers: Angela Mei, Bas Partovi, Brian Summers, Stephen Williams

The Trading Post allows users to both buy and sell textbooks to and from other users. The target setting
of this app would be a University campus, like UMD, and allows students to buy and sell textbooks from
other students. The app gives students a cheaper and faster option for their textbook needs than the
University Book Store and online retailers like Amazon. Students can barter buying and selling prices,
meetup times, and locations. This eliminates large price tags and shipping needs.

The Trading Post has been developed on Android 4.4.2 (API 19), uses Google Play Services, and uses
Azure Mobile Services to store user data and posts. The app was developed using the Eclipse IDE and
testing was performed on both emulators and a physical Android device.
